How It Works

The Edge
DraftKings sets odds that imply a probability for each outcome. Our model independently estimates the real probability. When the model says a team covers at 59% but DK's odds imply only 52%, that 7-point gap is the edge — you're getting a better price than the true odds suggest.
Kelly Criterion Sizing
Instead of betting the same amount on every pick, Kelly sizes each bet proportional to your edge. Bigger edge = bigger bet. A 9% edge gets roughly 3x the wager of a 3% edge. We use half-Kelly (conservative) to reduce volatility — you'll grow slower but survive losing streaks.
Safety Rails
Max 5% of bankroll on any single bet. Max 15% total daily exposure. Confirmed tanking teams get a 3% penalty. Back-to-back games get a 1.5-2.5% penalty. If a pick conflicts with sharp money movement, Kelly fraction drops 25%.
Bankroll Tracking
When you place bets, the wagered amount is immediately deducted from your available bankroll. Wins add back the wager + profit. Losses stay deducted. This means the number you see is always your real available cash.
All models carry uncertainty. Edges are probabilistic, not guarantees. Bet responsibly.
